11069 אנגלית טכנית יישומית – תוכנה
This English for Academic and Professional Purposes course is designed for software engineering students who aim to build confidence and competence in using English for real-world communication in academic, technological, and international work environments. Aligned with the B2+ level of the CEFR, the course emphasizes effective oral communication, presentation skills, teamwork, and intercultural awareness.
Through a combination of group discussions, simulations, and a virtual exchange with European university students, learners will enhance their ability to present technical content, engage in professional dialogue, and collaborate across cultures. Students will develop key life skills including scheduling, leadership, and follow-through, while also practicing oral reporting, academic discussions, and job interview techniques.
Assessment includes a final field-related group presentation, a job interview simulation, mini-presentations, and participation in an international virtual exchange. By the end of the course, students will be able to communicate ideas clearly and persuasively in English, work effectively in diverse teams, and apply creative problem-solving skills in academic and workplace scenarios.
